ABOUT US

EC FREIGHT Cargo is a limited liability company registered in DUBAI, UAE and having its own network in QATAR, Sultanate of Oman, Bahrain, Kuwait ,Soudia Arabia and India. Apart from own network we have association with world’s leading freight companies enabling us to provide high standards of service at a reasonable rates. Major portion of volume discounts availed from worldwide freight companies are passed on to our clients which makes us most cost effective service provider.

EC FREIGHT is a professionally managed organization with clearly defined areas of function and responsibility. The personnel occupying managerial positions have years of field experience and know very intimately the intricacies of cargo industry and sensitivity attached to client's goods. Company’s leadership quality can be envisaged by the fact that EC FREIGHT has had negligible employee turnover over the years.
